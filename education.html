Education

In the year 2000, when my family was at Moghbajar, Dhaka, my parents took me to the nearest kindergarten school. It was "Shahid Lieutenant Salim Shikkhaloy". I was only 4 at that time and was admitted into play group.

Later my family shifted to Mirpur. At that time I was 6 or 7 (I couldn’t remember), my parents admitted me to an english medium school named “Kingshuk Participatory School”. In 2004 I got myself admitted into “Monipur High School and College” in the class of 1. I read there for 2 years, class 1 & 2.

In December, 2005 my family moved to Satkhira, our home town. In January,2006 I took admission into “Satkhira Gov’t High School” and got myself admitted there in class 3. I was admitted there as a student for 8 years. In 2014, I attained SSC exam and I got GPA 5.00 out of 5.00 . Then I started higher secondary at “Satkhira Gov’t College”. In 2016, I attained HSC exam and ended up with GPA 4.58 out of 5.00 .

In December, 2016 I started my graduation in Accounting (Major) at National University of Bangladesh and still continuing.
